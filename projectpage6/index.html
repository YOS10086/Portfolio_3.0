Polyfinity Lamp

The Polyfinity Lamp is a moduar lamp design combining simple polyhedron geometry and reflective iridescent cellophane to create a radiating visual effect.

The design comprises of a polyhedral framework, with slots on each slide allowing for prisms of varying shapes and length to fit through, allowing the user to figure the lamp however they wish.

Process & Development

Development of the project began with material testing and testing out slot tolerances, befor emoving on to determining the final shape and dimensions.
